🛡️ AshGuard – AI Powered Women Safety & Travel Assistant

AshGuard is an AI-powered web application designed to ensure women's safety and provide smart travel assistance. It combines real-time emergency features, AI-based route safety analysis, and intelligent travel planning into a single platform.

Your Guardian Angel – Anytime, Anywhere.
